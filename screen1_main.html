Recommended Treatment Plan
Optimal treatment approach
Total Knee Arthroplasty (TKA)
Total Knee Replacement surgery is recommended as the most effective treatment for your condition. This procedure involves replacing the damaged knee joint surfaces with prosthetic components made of metal and plastic. The surgery will restore function, reduce pain, and improve your quality of life significantly.
Procedure Details:
Type: Primary Total Knee Replacement
Approach: Minimally Invasive Technique
Prosthesis: Advanced Ceramic-on-Polyethylene Implant
Anesthesia: Spinal/Epidural with Sedation
Duration: Approximately 1.5 – 2 hours
Hospital Stay: 3-4 days post-operative
Post-Operative Physiotherapy Program
A comprehensive physiotherapy rehabilitation program is essential for optimal recovery. The program will begin within 24 hours post-surgery and continue for 6-12 weeks, focusing on restoring strength, flexibility, and function to your knee joint.
Rehabilitation Timeline:
1
Week 1-2: Early Mobilization
Gentle range of motion exercises, walking with assistance, pain management
2
Week 3-6: Progressive Strengthening
Resistance exercises, balance training, gradual increase in activity
3
Week 7-12: Functional Recovery
Advanced exercises, stair climbing, return to daily activities
Additional Recommendations & Considerations
Pre-Operative Preparation
  • Complete blood tests and cardiac evaluation
  • Stop blood thinners 5 days before surgery
  • Attend pre-operative physiotherapy session
  • Arrange home care assistance for 2 weeks
Post-Operative Care
  • Daily physiotherapy sessions for first week
  • Pain management medication as prescribed
  • Blood clot prevention protocol
  • Follow-up appointments at 2, 6, and 12 weeks
Prescribed Medications
  • Pain relief: Tramadol 50mg as needed
  • Anti-inflammatory: Celecoxib 200mg daily
  • Blood thinner: Rivaroxaban 10mg daily
  • Antibiotic prophylaxis as per protocol
Expected Outcomes

  • Significant improvement in mobility
  • Return to normal daily activities in 3 months
  • Implant longevity: 15-20 years average
